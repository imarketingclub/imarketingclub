Welcome to iMarketing Club —
Here's Why I Built This
(And What You'll Get)

Another marketing blog. I know what you're thinking. Do we really need another one? Honestly — probably not. There are already thousands of them out there. But here's the thing: most of them are terrible.

And I mean that in the nicest possible way.

They're filled with vague advice written by people who've never actually run a campaign, built a list, or sweated over whether an email subject line was going to get opens or get ignored. They regurgitate the same SEO-friendly listicles that tell you to "create valuable content" and "build an audience" — without ever telling you how, or what "valuable" actually means when you're sitting there at 11pm wondering if any of this is going to work.

So Who Am I, Anyway?

My name is Jay Salton. I've been doing this — digital marketing, affiliate marketing, online business — for over a decade. I've bought hundreds of courses. I've tested more tools than I can remember. I've had campaigns that bombed spectacularly and a few that performed well beyond what I expected.

I'm not a "seven-figure guru." I'm not going to show you a screenshot of a PayPal account with a suspiciously round number and tell you that you can replicate it in 30 days if you just buy my course. That's not what this is.

What I am is someone who pays attention. Someone who tests things properly before forming opinions. Someone who knows the difference between a tool that genuinely helps and one that just has a great sales page. And someone who's spent years wishing there was a resource I could point beginners — and intermediate marketers — to and say: start here.

What iMarketing Club Is (And Isn't)

This site is built around three things.

First, honest tool reviews and recommendations. I've spent a ridiculous amount of money buying and testing digital marketing products over the years. Some are legitimately excellent. A lot are not. I'm going to tell you which is which — including linking you straight to the sales page so you can read it yourself and make your own call. I'll never recommend something I wouldn't use myself.

Second, the blog you're reading right now. No fluff. No filler. Long-form posts that actually respect your time and intelligence. I'll share what's working, what isn't, and why. I'll write about strategy, tools, mindset, and the mechanics of running an online marketing business — from someone who's in it every day.

Third, access. You can reach me directly via the contact page. I read every message. I don't promise to reply to everything — life is short and the inbox is deep — but if you've got a genuine question or something useful to share, I'm listening.

One Last Thing

The name iMarketing Club isn't accidental. "Club" is intentional. This isn't a broadcast. It's a community in the making — a gathering place for people who are serious about building something real online, who want straight information rather than hype, and who are willing to put in the work.

If that's you — welcome. You're in the right place.

Browse the tools. Read the posts. And if something resonates, reach out. Let's build something worth building.
