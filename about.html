Authoritativeness

Why iMarketing Club
Exists

I built this site because I was tired of searching for genuine, practical information about digital marketing tools and affiliate strategies — and finding mostly SEO-optimised fluff, recycled advice, and reviews written by people who'd clearly never actually used the product they were recommending.

"Every recommendation on this site is something I've used, tested, or have direct knowledge of from working in this industry."

That's the editorial standard. It sounds obvious, but it's rarer than it should be. The make-money-online niche is full of content designed to rank and earn commissions — not to actually help the person reading it. iMarketing Club is my attempt to do something different.

When I recommend a tool, I link to the sales page so you can make your own call. When something has changed or I've updated my opinion, I say so. When I have an affiliate relationship with a product, I disclose it. The goal is to be the resource I wished had existed when I started.

My Approach

What I Believe About
Building Online

Simplicity Beats Complexity

The most successful online businesses I've seen are built on a handful of things done consistently well — not twenty tools and a complicated system nobody can maintain.

Your List Is Your Business

Social platforms disappear, algorithms change, accounts get banned. Your email list is the one asset in digital marketing that you actually own. Build it first.

Honesty Compounds

Recommending bad products for a commission might pay once. Recommending only what you'd genuinely stand behind builds a reputation that pays forever. I'll take the second option.

Free Tools First

You can build a real affiliate marketing business using entirely free tools. Upgrade paid features when they genuinely unlock something meaningful — not before.
